# Privacy Policy for Glassora

**Last updated: June 2026**

Glassora is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Glassora wallpaper application (the "App"). We are designed to respect your personal information by collecting as little data as possible to deliver a fast, beautiful, and secure experience.

---

### 1. No Personal Data Collection
Glassora does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). You are not required to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information to browse, download, or use wallpapers.

There is no registration process, no user profiles, and no social login integration. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, export, or delete.

---

### 2. Cloud & Media Storage
We host our high-resolution wallpapers securely on Google Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage. When you browse or download a wallpaper, the request is processed anonymously — we do not track which device downloads which wallpaper, nor do we build user behavior profiles.

The Firebase SDK, provided by Google LLC, may automatically collect limited technical data (such as IP address and device identifiers) for service integrity, abuse prevention, and performance monitoring. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not access or use this technical data.

---

### 3. Device Permissions
Glassora requests the following device permissions:

* **Photo Library (Write-Only)**: To save downloaded wallpapers to your Photos library. We only write images — we never read, scan, browse, or access any existing photos in your library.
* **Push Notifications**: To send you local daily wallpaper reminder notifications. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using iOS's built-in notification system. They are **NOT** sent from any server — no notification tokens, device tokens, or identifiers are transmitted to us or any third party. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

---

### 4. Local Data Storage
Glassora stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

* Your favorite wallpapers list (up to 100 items)
* App preferences (theme, resolution, notification settings)
* Onboarding completion status
* Launch and download counts (for App Store rating prompts only)

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

---

### 5. Memory & Cache
To optimize loading speeds and reduce cellular data usage, wallpaper images are temporarily cached in the device's system memory (RAM) and on-disk URLCache (up to 300 MB). This cache is managed dynamically by your iOS device and is automatically cleared when system memory is needed or when you uninstall the App.

---

### 6. No Advertising & No Tracking
Glassora does not display any advertisements. We do not integrate any ad SDKs (such as AdMob, Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, or similar services).

We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Amplitude, Mixpanel, UXCam, or similar services). We do not collect or access your Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA), and we do not use the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to any third party for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

---

### 7. Children's Privacy
Since Glassora does not collect any personal data from any user, there is no special risk to children's privacy. The App does not knowingly solicit or collect data from children under the age of 13 (or any other age group). No age-gating mechanism is required because no personal information is collected or processed.

---

### 8. Security
All communication between the App and our cloud services is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Wallpaper data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We do not store any sensitive information on external servers.

---

### 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the App with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
